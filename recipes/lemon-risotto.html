Lemon Risotto

Description

An easy, hands-off recipe that will feed the whole family and have you clambering for more, all while you kick up your feet and enjoy 30 min to rest

Ingredients

  • 1 TBSP oil
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 clove diced garlic
  • 1 cup risotto
  • 3 cups boiling water with 1 chicken stock
  • 100g diced chicken
  • 1 cup peas
  • 2 TBSP lemon juice

Steps

  1. Heat oil then add onion and garlic in a crockpot.
  2. Once fragrant add chicken and cook until browned.
  3. Add the water and stock, followed by the risotto.
  4. Season to taste.
  5. Add peas and lemon juice.
  6. Place lid on the pot and put in the over at 180 degrees for 30 minutes.
