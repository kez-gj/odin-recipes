Shakshuka

Description

An quick but hearty meal, as low in calories as it is in effort

Ingredients

  • 1 TBSP oil
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 clove diced garlic
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tin of good quality tomatoes
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 cup frozen peppers

Steps

  1. Heat oil then add onion and garlic.
  2. Once fragrant add tinned tomatoes, spinach and peppers.
  3. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Crack 3 eggs into the tomato sauce and cover pan with lid.
  5. ACook until eggs are done to your liking (10-15min).
  6. Serve with naan.
